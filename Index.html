FAQ

Common Questions
Answered.

How long does a website take to build?

Most websites are delivered within 5–10 business days from the agreed start date. You will receive a clear timeline at the very beginning of your project.

Do I need to provide the content?

Ideally yes — you know your business best. If you need guidance I can help you structure your copy and advise on what each section needs. Photos can also be sourced.

What if I need changes after the site is live?

Small updates — text changes, new images, minor tweaks — are included in the monthly maintenance plan. Larger redesigns are quoted separately at a fair rate.

Can I cancel the maintenance plan?

Absolutely — no contracts, no lock-in periods. You can cancel at any time. Your website remains live; I simply stop managing it on your behalf.

What does maintenance cover exactly?

Monthly content updates, security patches, software updates, regular backups, performance checks, and priority support via email or WhatsApp — all for €12 per month.

How many revisions are included?

Every project includes 2 full rounds of revisions before launch. In practice, most clients are happy after the first version — but I want you completely satisfied.
